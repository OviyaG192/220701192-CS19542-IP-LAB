New Title IX Regulations: Colleges in several Southern states, including Georgia, Alabama, Florida, and South Carolina, are now required to implement the 2024 Title IX regulations starting from August 1. These regulations, issued by the U.S. Department of Education, aim to address sex discrimination and include new training requirements for all employees. The recent court decision has denied requests for a preliminary injunction to block these regulations, leaving colleges to comply promptly​ (JD Supra)​. 2024 College Rankings Released: U.S. News & World Report has published its 2024 college rankings. The rankings highlight changes and movements among various institutions, with some colleges seeing significant shifts. For example, the Air Force Academy and West Point are notable among the top-ranking military academies​ (College Kickstart)​. U.S. Naval War College Convocation: The U.S. Naval War College is set to commence its 2024-2025 academic year with a convocation ceremony on August 8. This event will welcome new students from various joint services, international militaries, and federal and state agencies. The college continues its tradition of providing rigorous education in national security and strategic studies​ (US Naval War College)​.
