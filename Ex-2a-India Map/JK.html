JAMMU KAHSMIR

Jammu and Kashmir, a region in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent, is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty and complex geopolitical significance. Nestled in the Himalayas, it boasts diverse landscapes, from lush valleys and serene lakes to snow-capped peaks. The region is known for its vibrant cultural heritage, with influences from Hinduism, Buddhism, and Islam shaping its traditions and architecture. Despite its picturesque setting, Jammu and Kashmir has been at the center of longstanding political disputes, primarily between India and Pakistan, with both nations claiming sovereignty over the area. This ongoing conflict has had profound implications for the region's social and economic development, making Jammu and Kashmir a focal point of international attention and diplomatic efforts.
